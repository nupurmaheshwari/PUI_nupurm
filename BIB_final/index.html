OUR HISTORY


Bhangra in the Burgh brings together the top bhangra teams from across the nation to compete on one stage in a night of music, dance, and South Asian culture. Since the competition started in 2007, it has only continued to grow in popularity and acclaim.

The competition, now deemed the largest student run event in Pittsburgh, has attracted a sold out crowd of 2,500 people for the past six years so it is no doubt that the art of bhangra is truly a sight to see.
